About Me

I am a passionate and result-driven Junior Data Analyst, dedicated to extracting meaningful insights from complex datasets and transforming them into actionable strategies. I have a Diploma in Data Science from TelOne Centre for Learning in Zimbabwe, and I'm currently pursuing an online Bachelors of Science in Business Intelligence and Information Systems with Osiri University in United States of America. With a solid foundation in mathematics, statistics, machine leanring, business studies, I leverage data-driven approaches to solve real-world problems and drive business success.

Throughout my career, I have worked on a variety of projects, from predictive modelling and data visualization to machine learning and AI-driven solutions. I take pride in my ability to communicate technical concepts in a clear and understandable way, making data accessible to all my stakeholders.

My expertise spans across data preprocessing, feature engineering, exploratory data analysis, and model deployment. I am proficient in tools and languages like Python, SQL, Tableau, Microsoft Power BI, Microsoft Excell, and I am eager to learn new technologies that can further enhance my work. Beyond technical skills, I am driven by a curiosity to understand the stories behind the numbers, and I am committed to turning raw data into insights that help businesses and organizations make informed decisions.

Thank you for exploring my portfolio. If you're interested in collaborating or discussing how data science can bring value to your organization, I would be happy to connect!
